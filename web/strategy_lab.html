03 Honest caveats — apply to ALL

• Bull-flattered: year-by-year the book LOSES in bear (Sharpe 2022 −0.8, 2026 −1.4). The regime filter (→cash) limits bear damage — not magic.
• Real DD ≈ 2× close DD (intrabar + leverage). At 2× lev, financing ~10% APR on notional; >2× risks liquidation.
• Survivorship: backtests use surviving coins → optimistic ceiling. #1 open fix = point-in-time universe.
• Funding NOT modeled: fees + slippage costed both sides, but NOT perp funding → any leveraged CAGR is optimistic. 1× spot is properly costed.
• The real gate is unmet: none has run live through a regime change (bull↔bear). That live survival — not any backtest — unlocks real money (3-6mo paper + one regime shift first).
• Honest live expectation: ~15–25% CAGR at moderate leverage, real DD ~2× — beats index, NOT magic.
