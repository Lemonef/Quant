Honest caveats (apply to ALL)

• Bull-flattered: year-by-year the book LOSES in bear (book Sharpe 2022 −0.8, 2026 −1.4) — the high OOS Sharpe leans on bull years. The regime filter (→cash) is what limits the bear damage, not magic.
• Real DD ≈ 2× close DD (intrabar + leverage). At 2× leverage, financing ~10% APR on notional; >2× risks liquidation. Plan for it.
• Survivorship: backtests use surviving coins → optimistic ceiling. The #1 open fix = point-in-time universe (the RTN-Core gating step).
• Funding NOT modeled: backtests cost fees + slippage both sides, but NOT perp funding — so any leveraged/futures CAGR is optimistic. 1× spot is properly costed.
• The real gate is unmet: none of these has yet run live through a REGIME CHANGE (bull↔bear). That live survival — not any backtest number — is what unlocks real money (target 3-6 months paper + one regime shift first).
• Honest live expectation: ~15–25% CAGR at moderate leverage with real DD ~2× — beats index, NOT magic. Paper-trade before real capital.
