01 Original thesis — for the record, didn't hold

Hold the strongest-trending liquid coins from a survivorship-bias-free universe, only when regime is risk-on, sized down when funding is hot — upgrading the Donchian/MA200 edge through better construction. The construction ideas (PIT universe, funding throttle) remain valid risk hygiene, but rotational selection itself does not beat the core.

02 Construction — what was built

Universe (PIT)Survivorship-free: listing-date gating (+30d), rolling 30d median $ADV ≥ ~$20M, delisting exit at last price, symbol-migration mapping, perp/funding availability for the levered version. Rebuilt weekly.
SignalRank eligible coins by trend strength (Donchian 55/20 breakout + normalized momentum). Hold top 8, equal-weight, 4H close. Rank-hysteresis (swap only if challenger beats a held coin by ≥2 ranks) to cut churn.
Regime gateBTC > MA200 = risk-on (the validated master filter that halved 2022 losses). Risk-off → cash.
SizingATR risk sizing (~0.5–1%/trade); funding-aware throttle (cut size when perp funding hot); leverage ≤1.5× core / ≤2× high-CAGR; per-coin ≤15%.
RiskDonchian-20 / ATR stop; max-DD guardrail de-grosses on breach.

03 Known risks — why it was always fragile

Rotational top-N collapses into overfit cross-sectional momentum without strict walk-forward + regime gating · N / ranking-blend / ADV threshold are tunable knobs = overfit surface · 4H closes can't see liquidation-wick deleveraging · the universe is the biggest single risk — if it isn't truly PIT, everything downstream inherits the artifact.
